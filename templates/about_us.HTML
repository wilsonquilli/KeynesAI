About the Devs

Keynes AI is inspired by the ideas of John Maynard Keynes, one of the most influential economists of the 20th century. Known for his insights into market psychology and economic behavior, Keynes believed that emotions and human behavior often drive markets more than logic. Our goal with Keynes AI is to bring clarity to that chaos by using modern artificial intelligence to analyze market data, trends, and sentiment. By doing so, the platform helps investors make smarter decisions, reduce guesswork, and stay ahead of market shifts with reliable, data-driven insights.
